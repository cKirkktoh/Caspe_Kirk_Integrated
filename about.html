About Me

I am Kirk Caspe, who really loves taking photos
especially nature. You can discover beauty in the world
through photography. Finding beauty in the
things and people around us by using a camera helps
us to view things differently.

I was born at Jeddah, Saudi Arabia and grew and lived there for 17 years
before moving back to the Philippines to study at my last senior high school
year. Before finishing my final high school year, at December 8th, we flew to
Canada. My parents sacrificed their jobs at Saudi just to give me and my little
brother to have a bright future ahead of us.

Not only I am into photography but I am really a sports person.
Especially basketball and football but some would call it, "Soccer".

Well not only sports is my interest but travelling is my number one hobby.
Ever since me and my family first travelled to Hong Kong for vacation when I
was a kid, I already knew at that point that travelling would make escape
reality. And year after year, after finishing each of our school years, we travelled
to so many places. Here are some of the countries I've visited in order.
  • Hong Kong (2012)
  • Singapore (2013)
  • UAE (2014)
  • South Korea (2015)
  • Japan (2018)
  • Spain (2018)
  • Italy (2018)
  • Switzerland (2018)
  • Belgium (2018)
  • Netherlands (2018)
  • France (2018)

Ever since I put my foot at the heart of Tokyo, I pulled out my phone just to
take photos and I, was amazed by my shot. That is the day, I became interested in
photography. Taking photos of the breathtaking nature of this world, the beautiful
skyscrapers of the city, and the captivating landmarks of each place is my favorite
thing to do.
